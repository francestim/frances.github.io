    College days made it a bit hard to play video games, with how stacked your schedule could be, sleep perhaps becomes a more luxurious commodity than other niceties nowadays. Although there are instances when I can push myself to how many extra hours of the day that I can just to play a video game or two for a bit, aside from also building model kits or spending the day doing nothing but browsing the internet during free days, it was the Switch hybrid console that pushed me to do it. Because of how it was advertised to be (not only being a console that you can hook up to the TV but a handheld to be played anywhere) it just gave me more reasons to play just because I can actively laze in bed whilst playing. I wouldn’t care if I had the most powerful console in my room, If I can't play it whilst in bed then what's the point, for me personally. So once in a while I could go for an hour or three because of the console that Nintendo created. Besides the known and obvious mistakes from Nintendo I see this as a solid and relatively cheap next gen device.
  Rating
   As stated I recommend this console to anyone. Aside from the known mistakes from the Nintendo company that they were known to make during this consoles time, the library that Nintendo was able to build could accommodate anyones’ tastes, if they aren’t so stingy about factors like performance or graphics. This is a solid system.
