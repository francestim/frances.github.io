    My College IT program has shown me that working on the little screen of a laptop deems to be nigh impossible, especially with the constant coding and web designing that is needing to be done even simultaneously, that's where the monitor comes in, the KOORUI 24N2A to solve this dire IT need. And it has served well so far especially if you are on a budget, at only about a ₱4,000 - 5000 price range this 23 - 25 inch screen can work well with most setup, has a slick design, and has no problems whatsoever with school or office tasks. One issue I've had that was quickly fixed although was the constant blacking out of the screen when the HDMI cord was hit just a bit, you would have to make sure that the cord won't even touch anything that could squeeze it. Another issue, or perhaps just a nitpick, is that while gaming it isn't at full potential speeds than it should be, many factors could be the reason. One example could be that my mid-tier laptop is just not as capable of running 2 monitors at the same time, which is the most plausible case meaning it would not be a monitor issue. But nevertheless, this monitor has served me properly as my 1st external monitor when it comes to school/ office works.
  Rating
   Although the gaming experience is left to be desired, this is a perfect monitor for work purposes, being able to see things much more cleraly with a 23 - 25 screen of this caliber.
