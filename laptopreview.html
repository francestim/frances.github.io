    My very first gaming laptop handed by me by my parents. Like a Toyota Corolla, this laptop (from my experience) never suffered major damage no matter what was thrown through this thing, impressive for being a budget laptop and on that was also created back in 2019, I had really fond memories of it. Unto the technical side of things, the mid tier GPU and CPU really shine when it comes triple AAA games back in 2016, although I suggest, to mitigate heat even for a bit so that temps dont reach 90C and above at constant gaming, to make sure to have almost every graphical option from low to mid if you are not much of a stickler when it comes to graphics as this is a mid-tier gaming laptop.
  Rating
   All in all the Nitro 7 has impressed me due to its longetivity, and endurance. Not only that but its mid-tier quality gaming even able to push through some of the latest triple AAA games but in very low settings.
