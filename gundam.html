My Collection

  A slight background to my addiction.

    More than about 40 Gundams have been made by me so far all the way back to the disheartening pandemic. These Gundams or Gunpla (Gundam Plastic Models) hold a special place in my heart during those unfortunate times where panic were all over the news. It provided me a little solice for myself to quietly build and create something out of small things. Perhaps, it gives me inspiration leading up to my future endeavors, to build something out of my small somethings in life.
